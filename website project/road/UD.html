UI/UX Designer Roadmap

Stage 1: Basics

  • Learn design principles & color theory
  • Understand typography & layout
  • Introduction to design tools (Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD)

Stage 2: Intermediate

  • User research & wireframing
  • Prototyping & interaction design
  • Usability testing & feedback analysis

Stage 3: Advanced

  • Design systems & component libraries
  • Responsive & accessible design
  • Collaboration with developers & stakeholders

Stage 4: Expert

  • Lead UX projects & design strategy
  • Advanced prototyping & motion design
  • Mentor juniors & advocate for user-centered design
